Banana Oatmeal

Description

A Super simple oatmeal recipe with lots of healthy fats.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup quick oats
  • 1/2 cup soymilk
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/4 cup almonds
  • 10 walnuts
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • Optional: honey or maple syrup

Instructions

  1. In a small pot, mash up half of the banana.
  2. Add oatmeal and soymilk into the pot and mix.
  3. Cook on medium heat until oatmeal thickens a bit.
  4. Add in almonds and walnuts to warm up. Add extra milk if you prefer a thinner consistency.
  5. Serve in a bowl and top with other half of banana, pumpkin seeds, and honey or maple syrup.

Note: I use soymilk because it has more protein than other plantmilks but you can sub milk of your choice.
