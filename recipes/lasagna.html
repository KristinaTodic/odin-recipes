Lasagna

Description

A delicious and simple lasagna recipe that is easy and quick to make.

This is a fully plant-based recipe using vegan ground meat alternatives and an easy to make tofu ricotta. You can substitute the vegan grounds with cooked beans, lentils, or soaked TVP!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Yves veggie grounds
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 jar or can of your favourite pasta sauce
  • No-boil lasagna noodles
  • Tofu ricotta:
    • 1 block extra firm tofu
    • juice of 1 lemon
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
    • 1/2 cup unsweetened soymilk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Finely chop onion and combine in bowl with veggie grounds
  3. Prepare tofu ricotta: combine all ingredients in a blender. Add more soymilk if needed until desired consistency is reached.
  4. Assemble lasagna by starting with a 9x9in pan.
  5. Add enough pasta sauce to cover bottom of pan.
  6. Place noodles on top of pasta sauce.
  7. Spoon a generous amount of tofu ricotta on top of the noodles.
  8. Spread a decent layer of onion veggie ground mix on top.
  9. Repeat steps above til all of the grounds and ricotta are used up
  10. Cover pan with tinfoil and bake for 45 minutes.
  11. After 45 minutes remove tinfoil and add vegan cheese on top. Return to oven for 15 more minutes.

And that's it! Serve and enjoy!
